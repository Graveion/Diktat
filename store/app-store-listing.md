## Description (≤4000 chars)

Diktat is a remote control for the AI coding sessions running on your Mac.

A small companion runs on your Mac and holds your Claude Code and Cursor CLI
sessions. Diktat connects to it securely, so you can start, resume, and steer
real coding work from your phone — on the couch, on a walk, or away from your
desk.

HOW IT WORKS
• Install the free Mac companion with one command, then pair by scanning a QR
  code shown in your terminal.
• Your Mac dials out to the Diktat relay — nothing on your machine is exposed to
  the internet, and there are no ports to open.
• Open the app, pick your Mac, and you're in your sessions.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Resume any existing Claude Code or Cursor session exactly where you left it.
• Start new coding tasks and watch the agent work in real time.
• Dictate instructions by voice — speak a change instead of typing on a phone
  keyboard.
• Get a push notification the moment a run finishes, so you can kick off a long
  task and walk away.
• Switch between multiple Macs and projects from one place.

BUILT FOR PEOPLE WHO ALREADY LIVE IN THE CLI
Diktat doesn't replace Claude Code or Cursor — it drives the sessions you already
have, with the logins you already use. If it runs in your terminal, you can reach
it from your phone.

WHAT YOU NEED
• A Mac with the free Diktat companion installed
• Claude Code and/or the Cursor CLI, installed and logged in

PRICING
Your first hour is free. After that, Diktat Pro is an auto-renewing subscription:
• Monthly — $3.99/month
• Annual — $29.99/year, with a 7-day free trial

Payment is charged to your Apple ID at purchase confirmation. Subscriptions renew

the current period; your account is charged for renewal within 24 hours of the
end of the current period. Manage or cancel anytime in your App Store account
settings. Any unused portion of a free trial is forfeited when you buy a
subscription.
