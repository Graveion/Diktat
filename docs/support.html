Support

Need a hand? Most things are quick to sort out — and you can always email us.

Email support@diktat.app and we'll get back to you. Including your device model and iOS version helps us help you faster.

Getting started

  • Install on your Mac. Run the one-line installer from the Diktat home page, then diktat setup to detect your coding tools.
  • Pair your phone. Run diktat pair on your Mac — it shows a QR code. In the app, tap Pair a machine and scan it.
  • Start working. Run diktat start, open the app, pick your Mac, and start a session.

Troubleshooting

  • My Mac shows as offline. Make sure the daemon is running with diktat status; start it with diktat start. Check ~/.diktat/daemon.log for errors.
  • Pairing didn't work. QR codes expire after a few minutes — run diktat pair again for a fresh one.
  • No coding tools detected. Make sure Claude Code or the Cursor CLI is installed, on your PATH, and logged in, then re-run diktat setup.
  • Voice isn't transcribing. Grant microphone and speech-recognition permission to Diktat in iOS Settings.

Subscriptions

  • Diktat starts with one free hour of use; after that it's a subscription.
  • Manage or cancel anytime in Settings → your name → Subscriptions on your iPhone. Subscriptions are billed through your Apple ID.
  • Have a complimentary code? Enter it on the paywall under Redeem code.

Account & privacy
