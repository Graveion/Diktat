Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 June 2026

Diktat lets you drive coding sessions running on your own Mac from your phone. We collect as little as possible, and we don't sell your data or use it for advertising. This policy explains what we handle and why.

What we collect

  • Account identity. When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive a user identifier and your email address (Apple may relay a private address). This is used only to create and authenticate your account.
  • Machine registry. When you pair a Mac, we store a machine name you choose, a hashed per-machine access token, and a "last seen" timestamp so the app can show which machines are online.
  • Subscription status. Purchases are processed by Apple. We store only your entitlement state (trial, complimentary access, or active subscription) so we know whether to unlock the app. Subscription processing uses RevenueCat.
  • Voice input. If you use voice, speech is transcribed to text on your device using Apple's speech-recognition services. We store the resulting instructions only as part of your coding session; we do not store raw audio.

Your coding sessions

Instructions you send and the output from your coding tools pass through our relay in transit between your phone and your Mac. The relay is a pass-through broker — it does not persist the contents of your sessions. The actual code, files, and tool activity stay on your own machine.

Service providers

We use a small number of processors to run the service:

  • Supabase — authentication and account/machine database.
  • Fly.io — hosting for the relay that connects your phone to your Mac.
  • RevenueCat and Apple — subscription processing.
  • Apple / Google — sign-in.
  • Expo — delivery of push notifications (e.g. when a run finishes).

Data retention & deletion

You can permanently delete your account at any time from the app (Your machines → Delete account). This erases your account, paired machines, pairing codes, and stored entitlement state. Deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.

Children

Diktat is a developer tool and is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we'll update the date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.
